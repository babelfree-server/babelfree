Document translation

Accurate, certified document translation services for legal, academic, business, medical, and immigration purposes. Every document is handled by subject-matter experts who understand the terminology and the stakes involved.

Types of documents we translate

Document translation demands more than language fluency. It requires deep familiarity with the conventions, formats, and regulatory expectations of each document type. At Babel Free, we assign every project to a translator whose expertise matches the subject matter, ensuring that the translated document carries the same authority and precision as the original.

Our document translation services span a wide range of categories, each with its own requirements and standards:

  • Legal documents: Contracts, court filings, affidavits, power of attorney, intellectual property filings, and compliance documentation. Legal translation requires exact terminology and an understanding of the legal frameworks in both the source and target jurisdictions.
  • Academic documents: Diplomas, transcripts, research papers, dissertations, and institutional correspondence. Academic translation preserves the formal register and citation standards required by universities and accreditation bodies worldwide.
  • Business documents: Financial reports, corporate bylaws, shareholder agreements, audit statements, and internal policies. Business translation requires fluency in both language and corporate conventions.
  • Medical documents: Patient records, clinical trial documentation, pharmaceutical labeling, informed consent forms, and regulatory submissions. Medical translation demands absolute accuracy, as errors can have serious consequences for patient safety and regulatory compliance.
  • Immigration documents: Birth certificates, marriage certificates, police clearances, visa applications, and supporting evidence packages. Immigration translation must meet the formatting and certification requirements of the receiving authority.

No matter the document type, we maintain strict confidentiality throughout the process. All files are handled through secure channels, and our translators adhere to non-disclosure agreements as standard practice.

Our translation process

A reliable translation is the product of a disciplined process. We have refined our workflow over years of professional practice to eliminate ambiguity and deliver consistent results at every stage.

Step 1: Project review. When you submit a document, our project managers assess its scope, complexity, and subject matter. We identify the appropriate translator based on language pair, domain expertise, and any certification requirements. At this stage, we also confirm the deadline and provide a transparent quote with no hidden fees.

Step 2: Translation. A subject-matter expert translates your document, preserving the meaning, tone, and formatting of the original. For legal and official documents, the translator follows the conventions expected by the target jurisdiction or institution. Technical terminology is cross-referenced against established glossaries to ensure consistency.

Step 3: Proofreading and review. A second linguist reviews the completed translation against the source document. This independent review catches errors that even experienced translators can overlook: inconsistent terminology, formatting discrepancies, or subtle shifts in meaning. For certified translations, the reviewer also verifies that all certification requirements are met.

Step 4: Delivery. The finalized document is delivered in your preferred format, whether that is a formatted PDF, an editable Word file, or a certified hard copy. If your document requires notarization or apostille, we can coordinate those additional steps as well.

Certified translations and quality assurance

Many institutions and government agencies require certified translations, which means the translator or translation agency attests to the accuracy and completeness of the translation with a signed declaration. Babel Free provides certified translations that are accepted by courts, immigration authorities, universities, and regulatory bodies across the world.

Our certification process follows the standards expected in the target country. In the United States, this typically means a signed Certificate of Translation Accuracy accompanying the translated document. In the European Union, we follow the specific requirements of each member state, including sworn translation where applicable. For other jurisdictions, we research and comply with local requirements before beginning work.

Quality assurance is embedded at every stage, not applied as an afterthought. Our translators use translation memory tools to ensure terminological consistency across large documents and multi-part projects. Every translation passes through at least one independent review before delivery. For high-stakes documents such as patent applications or clinical trial submissions, we offer an additional specialist review by a third linguist with domain-specific credentials.

We also maintain detailed project records, so if you need a revision or an updated version of a previously translated document, we can ensure consistency with the original translation.

Language pairs and turnaround times

Babel Free offers document translation across a broad range of language pairs, with particular depth in Spanish, English, French, German, Portuguese, and Italian. We also serve less common language combinations through our extended network of vetted professional translators. If you have a specific language pair in mind, contact us and we will confirm availability and pricing.

Turnaround times depend on the length and complexity of the document. As a general guide, a standard business document of up to 2,000 words can be completed within two to three business days. Longer or more specialized documents, such as legal contracts or technical manuals, may require five to ten business days. For urgent requests, we offer expedited turnaround at an additional fee, with delivery possible within 24 hours for shorter documents.

We are transparent about timelines from the start. When you request a quote, we provide a firm delivery date along with the price. If your project has a non-negotiable deadline, let us know up front and we will structure the workflow accordingly. We do not sacrifice quality for speed, but we are experienced at managing tight schedules without compromising accuracy.

Volume discounts are available for ongoing projects and large document sets. If your organization regularly needs translated documents, we can establish a standing arrangement with pre-agreed rates and priority scheduling.
