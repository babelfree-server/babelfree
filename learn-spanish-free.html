CEFR-aligned progress

Our Spanish language classes follow the Common European Framework of Reference for Languages (CEFR), the international standard for language proficiency. Your progress is structured across six levels:

  • A1 — Breakthrough — basic greetings, simple questions, everyday objects
  • A2 — Waystage — routine conversations, describing your environment, expressing needs
  • B1 — Threshold — holding conversations, expressing opinions, narrating events
  • B2 — Vantage — fluent interaction, understanding complex texts, debating ideas
  • C1 — Effective Operational — nuanced expression, professional contexts, literary appreciation
  • C2 — Mastery — near-native fluency, creative expression, effortless comprehension

Because our course follows CEFR, your progress is internationally recognized. Whether you need Spanish for travel, work, university admissions, or personal enrichment, your achievements here map directly to recognized proficiency levels.
