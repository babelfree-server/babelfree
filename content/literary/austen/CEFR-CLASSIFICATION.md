## KEY INSIGHT
Austen is the **irony** author. Where Wilde gives paradox and Twain gives vernacular satire, Austen gives **irony so perfect it looks like simplicity**. "It is a truth universally acknowledged" is six words that mean the opposite of what they say. That's B2 to recognize and C1 to fully unpack.
