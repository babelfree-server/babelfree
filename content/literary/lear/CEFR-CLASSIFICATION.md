## KEY INSIGHT
Lear is the **nonsense** author — but nonsense is not nothing. Where Carroll gives logic puzzles in Wonderland, Lear gives **sound as joy** and **melancholy dressed as silliness**. The Dong and Yonghy-Bonghy-Bo are as heartbreaking as any Victorian lyric. Limericks at B1, narrative poems at B2, the great melancholy poems at C1. He pairs naturally with Carroll (both British nonsense) but adds the emotional dimension Carroll withholds.
