## KEY INSIGHT
Kipling is the **adventure** author. Where Poe gives gothic, Carroll gives logic, Dickinson gives compression — Kipling gives **the animal world as moral universe**. The Law of the Jungle is a legal system. Mowgli's exile is an identity crisis. The Jungle Book teaches B1 students through action and teaches C1 students through allegory.
